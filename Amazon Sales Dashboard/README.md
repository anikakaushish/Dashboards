Overview -->
Developed an interactive sales dashboard to analyze Amazon product sales performance, customer reviews, and product category trends.

Key Insights -->
 * Tracked YTD sales, products sold, and customer reviews
 * Analyzed monthly and weekly sales trends
 * Identified top-performing products by sales and reviews
 * Compared sales performance across product categories

Features -->
 * KPI Cards
 * Sales Trend Analysis
 * Product Category Analysis
 * Interactive Filters & Slicers
 * Top Product Performance Visualization
